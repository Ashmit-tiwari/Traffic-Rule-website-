📚 Learn Traffic Rules

Understand • Learn • Apply

🚦 Traffic Signals

🔴 Red Signal

Stop your vehicle completely behind the stop line. Never cross the intersection until the signal turns green.

🟡 Yellow Signal

Slow down and prepare to stop safely. Avoid accelerating to beat the signal.

🟢 Green Signal

Move ahead only after checking that the road is clear and safe.

🚧 Important Road Signs

🛑 Stop Sign

Always stop completely before moving forward.

🚫 No Entry

Vehicles are not allowed to enter this road from your direction.

🏫 School Zone

Reduce your speed and stay alert for children crossing the road.

🦺 Safety Rules

🪖 Wear a Helmet

Helmets protect riders from serious head injuries and should always be fastened properly.

💺 Wear a Seat Belt

Every passenger in the vehicle should wear a seat belt, including those in the back seat.

📵 Avoid Mobile Phones

Never use your phone while driving. It distracts you and increases accident risk.

🚗 Speed Limits

Always drive within the prescribed speed limit. Overspeeding is one of the leading causes of road accidents.

🚑 Emergency Vehicles

Give immediate way to ambulances, fire brigade vehicles, and police emergency vehicles whenever you hear their sirens.

🚶 Pedestrian Safety

Always use zebra crossings and obey pedestrian traffic signals before crossing the road.
