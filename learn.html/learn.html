📚 Learn Traffic Rules

Understand • Learn • Apply

🚦 Traffic Signals

🔴 Red Light

Stop your vehicle completely before the stop line.

🟡 Yellow Light

Slow down and prepare to stop safely.

🟢 Green Light

Proceed only when the road ahead is clear.

🚧 Important Road Signs

🛑 Stop

Come to a complete stop before moving.

🚫 No Entry

Vehicles are not allowed beyond this point.

⚠ School Zone

Drive slowly and watch for children.

🦺 Safety Rules

Helmet

Always wear a helmet while riding a two-wheeler.

Seat Belt

Every passenger should wear a seat belt.

No Mobile Phone

Avoid using mobile phones while driving.

🚗 Speed Limits

Drive within the prescribed speed limit. Overspeeding increases the chances of accidents.

🚑 Emergency Vehicles

Always give way to ambulances, fire brigades and police emergency vehicles.
